Favorite Sanrio Character Picker

Use this picker to narrow down who (or what!) your favorite Sanrio characters are!

This site is still a work in progress, so if you want to save & share your favorites you’ll have to either:

  • take a screenshot while on your phone
  • hit PrtSc (Print Screen) on your keyboard (PC/Laptop) and “paste” it into either MSPaint or a photo editing software of your choice.

This picker is based off the “Favorite Pokemon Picker” by GitHub user antialiasis.

Images taken from various sources, including sanrio.co.jp & the hello kitty fandom wiki. With one or two minor edits by yours truly.
